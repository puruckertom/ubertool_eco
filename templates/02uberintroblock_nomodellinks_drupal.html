  • Models for Pesticide Risk Assessment

    When EPA assesses the risk of a pesticide to the environment, it considers the toxicity of the pesticide as well as the amount of pesticide to which the environment may be exposed. In assessing exposure, scientists frequently use mathematical models to predict pesticide concentrations in soil, sediment, air, food and water.
